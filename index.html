About Me

I am a musician, mixed media, and digital artist. I sometimes teach technology and art at various Universities and colleges. I am orginally from Washington DC, appropriately nicknamed, Hollywood for Ugly People. I love politics, the news, reading fiction and non fiction, and getting beat in scrabble by my wife who happens to be a scrabble champion. My main goal in life right now is supporting my twins with lots of love, and building a savings account to get them through the UC University system. I tend to have numerous projects going on at once. I created a music and arts festival in San Francisco that lasted about 15 years. I love technology, creating websites, music, and now learning code.
